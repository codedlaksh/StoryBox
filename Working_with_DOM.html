Stories from a Kabooliwala..

Packing their backups, crafting big surprises for their grandparents, finally starting with their parents. All those dreams during travel.Felling asleep on mother's lap. And finally, wakeup when your grandpa calls your petname. Wandering with him through the village.Eating those sweets from small shops of village hearts which were the most delicious things he ever had. Playing with new friends. Learning swimming from Grandpa.Hearing his childhood stories, hearing grandma's epic stories, hearing friend's blunder stories..They do miss all these..so this is for them..
